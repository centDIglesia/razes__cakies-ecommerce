About Us

Balancing the demands of school, social life, and business was a daunting challenge for Jannah Glaze Pangilinan. It all started with a heartfelt gesture-she wanted to give her mom a special birthday gift but she had no money to afford one. Instead, she decided to bake a cake. As she mixed the ingredients and crafted something from scratch, she fell in love with the process.
Later, when Jannah was tasked with a final project that required baking cakes and pastries, she saw it as a chance to dive deeper into this newfound passion. She shared her creations with her relatives and her boyfriend, and their praise fed all her hardwork and dedication in perfecting her craft. They suggested she start selling her cakes, believing in her talent even when she doubted herself.
The journey was far from easy. Jannah had spent countless hours in the kitchen, testing recipes and wasting ingredients in order to achieve perfection. Her boyfriend and relatives became her unofficial taste tester, providing valuable feedback that guided her efforts.
Finally, with tons of trial and error, Jannah perfected her recipes. The approval from everyone around her marked the beginning of something new. Jannah had turned her passion into a reality, fueled by perseverance and the support of those she loves the most.
